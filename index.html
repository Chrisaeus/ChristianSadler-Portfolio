About Me

I'm an artist and developer from the Pacific Northwest. I'm currently residing in South King County, in the greater Seattle area, but I've lived in various locations around the Puget Sound in Washington State and Oregon's Willamette Valley. I recieved a Bachelor of Science degree in Audio & Media Design Technology from the Art Institute of Seattle in 2018.

    My goal as a budding developer is to eventually combine my audio knowledge with my coding skills in order to create music- and audio-centered applications, plug-ins for Digital Audio Workstations, and program audio and music for video games both on consoles and mobile phones.

    When I'm not coding, I like writing raps and creating hip-hop music. When I'm not being creative I listen to hip-hop and metal music, watch horror movies, and play RPGs.
